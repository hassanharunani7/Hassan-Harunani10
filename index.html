About Me

I'm Hassan Harunani, a Tanzanian national swimmer, student leader, and aspiring forensic scientist. Born in the United States on April 26, 2010, and raised in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania from infancy, I've developed a unique global perspective through my connection to Tanzania, Kenya, and the United States.

Currently, I'm completing my IGCSE studies at Aga Khan Mzizima Secondary School with ambitious goals of achieving top grades and continuing to A-Level studies in Mathematics, Chemistry, and Biology. My ultimate dream is to pursue a degree in Biochemistry and specialize in Forensic Science as a forensic DNA analyst.

Swimming Career

A Decade of Dedication

Swimming has been one of the defining aspects of my life. I began at just three years old and have spent over a decade training with BlueFins Swimming Club in Dar es Salaam. This journey has been more than just competition—it's been a path of discipline, resilience, and continuous self-improvement.

Key Achievements:

  • 100+ Regional and National Medals
  • Multiple Trophies at National Competitions
  • International Representation at Africa Aquatics Zone III Competition in Burundi
